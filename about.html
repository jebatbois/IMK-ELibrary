Profile of Maritime University of Raja Ali Haji E-Library

History of Maritime University of Raja Ali Haji E-Library

As the Maritime University of Raja Ali Haji (UMRAH) continues to grow as a leading maritime-based higher education institution in Tanjungpinang, Riau Islands, the demand for broader academic information access has also increased. To meet this need, the Maritime University of Raja Ali Haji E-Library was established as a digital library designed to facilitate students, lecturers, and researchers in accessing academic resources easily and flexibly.

As part of UMRAH’s library system, this E-Library supports the management of digital collections from various faculties, including: Faculty of Engineering and Maritime Technology, Faculty of Economics and Business Faculty of Social and Political Sciences Faculty of Teacher Training and Education Faculty of Fisheries and Marine Science Faculty of Medicine With an integrated system, the Maritime University of Raja Ali Haji E-Library serves as a primary digital information hub for the academic community, supporting learning, research, and knowledge development.

Role and Principles of Maritime University of Raja Ali Haji E-Library The Maritime University of Raja Ali Haji E-Library plays a crucial role in supporting academic activities, research, and community engagement through innovative digital-based services. With a continuously expanding collection, including e-books, scientific journals, and online databases, the E-Library aims to be a key reference center for the academic community and the public. Currently, the UMRAH Library is located at Dompak Campus, near various key academic facilities of the university.
